Beach Packing List

  • Reef Safe Sunscreen (sunscreen without oxybenzone)
  • Towels
  • Reusable Water Bottle
  • DO NOT take lava rocks with you wherever you are and go on the islands

Be Plastic Free

Especially going to the beach and in general, try to limit your single-use plastic consumption as much as possible. When possible, use reusable bags and opt out of asking for plastic utensils and straws. We don't want to see the plastic find its way to the beach and clutter the shore.
